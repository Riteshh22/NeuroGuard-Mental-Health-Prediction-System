Help Guides

Welcome to Neuroguard

Neuroguard is designed to be your serene companion for mental well-being, providing insights into your emotional state and offering guided therapeutic exercises.

Taking Assessments

Tap the Check-in center button on the bottom menu to start. Answer honestly to build an accurate mental health blueprint.

Understanding Your Results

After an assessment, you will see a detailed breakdown. Scores update your profile level and streak. A critical tag indicates you may need to speak to a professional.

Tracking Progress

Visit the Home dashboard to track your Daily Streak, Energy levels, and Consistency.

Exercises & Support

The Support tab offers guided breathing, grounding, and meditation designed to provide immediate relief in moments of distress.

FAQ

What does the app do?

Neuroguard provides a safe space to track your daily mood, understand your mental health through structured assessments, and engage in therapeutic exercises.

How accurate are predictions?

Insights are based on established mental health frameworks, but they are not a medical diagnosis. Always consult a healthcare professional for clinical advice.

Is my data safe?

Yes. Your assessment responses are stored securely and privately on your device. We never sell your personal information.

How often should I check in?

For best results and to build a strong streak, we recommend taking an assessment once daily. However, you can check in whenever you need emotional clarity.

What if my risk level is high?

If your results show critical distress, we urge you to use the resources in the results screen to contact a mental health professional immediately.
